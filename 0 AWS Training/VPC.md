## Basics

  - CIDR (Classless Inter-Domain Routing)
    - `172.31.0.0/16`
      - a total of 32-bit address system (four binary octets)
      - `/16`: subnet mask

  RFC1918
    - `10.0.0.0` (10/8 prefix)
    - `172.16.0.0` (172.16/12 prefix) 
    - `192.168.0.0` (192.168/16 prefix)
